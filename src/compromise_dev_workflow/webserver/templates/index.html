Welcome to codetrov


Here's an ugly web page to uploade your garbo. Based off of https://docs.codecov.io/docs/about-the-codecov-bash-uploader

Upload YOUR test results with this simple one-liner.

bash <(curl -s http://localhost/bash)

In order to upload a file, you must set your $TROVTOKEN environment variable to the token supplied with your account. Remember: Do NOT share this with ANYBODY.

Upload one or multiple test reports by setting a $TROVUPLOADDIR environment variable describing which directory to upload.
